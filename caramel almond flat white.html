The Tall Grande and Venti nomenclature at Starbucks is derived from the Italian language. The word "Tall" refers to the size of the drink, "Grande" means large, and "Venti" means twenty. This naming convention was established in order to provide clarity for customers ordering drinks in a foreign language.. Everything You Need to Know About Starbucks Coffee Sizes Venti. Here's where we veer off course a little further because a "Venti" size at Starbucks means 20 ounces for hot drinks and 24 for cold. ("Venti" means "20" in Italian.) The Venti is equivalent to a large in "standard" sizing.

Ingredients:

  1. Espresso shots: Typically, a Flat White contains two shots of espresso.
  2. Steamed milk: Whole milk is traditionally used for a Flat White, but you can also use almond milk for the almond flavor.
  3. Caramel syrup: You'll need caramel syrup for sweetness and flavor.
  4. Almond syrup or almond extract: To enhance the almond flavor, you can use almond syrup or a few drops of almond extract.
  5. Caramel drizzle (optional): For an extra touch of caramel flavor and decoration.

    6. How Is It Made?

    Pull two shots of espresso using the espresso machine. Steam the milk until it reaches a velvety texture. If using almond milk, ensure it's properly steamed to create a creamy consistency. Add caramel syrup and almond syrup (or almond extract) to the bottom of your coffee cup.Pour the steamed milk over the syrups, holding back the foam with a spoon. Gently pour the espresso shots over the milk and syrups, allowing the espresso to blend with the milk. Spoon a dollop of foam on top of the drink. Optionally, drizzle caramel syrup on top of the foam for extra flavor and decoration. Your Caramel Almond Flat White coffee is now ready to enjoy! Adjust the amount of syrup according to your taste preferences.
