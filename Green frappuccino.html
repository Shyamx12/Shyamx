Green Frappuccino

A Green Tea Cream Frappuccino is a blended beverage that starts with a base of finely ground green tea powder, known as matcha. This vibrant green powder is made from specially grown and processed green tea leaves, giving it a distinct flavor and a concentrated dose of antioxidants. When combined with milk, ice, and a creamy base, the matcha creates a smooth and frothy mixture that forms the heart of the Green Tea Cream Frappuccino.

Ingredients:

  1. Matcha Green Tea Powder
  2. Milk
  3. Ice
  4. Sweetener
  5. Whipped Cream

How Is It Made?

To make a Green Tea Cream Frappuccino, the barista starts by blending the matcha powder, milk, ice, and sweetener until the mixture is smooth and creamy. The blended beverage is then poured into a cup and topped with a generous swirl of whipped cream. The result is a visually stunning drink with layers of vibrant green and fluffy white, inviting you to take a sip and experience the harmonious blend of flavors and textures.
