Strawberry Frappuccino

This is coffee-free and it would be a great choice for kids. After drinking it, I would say that it was more milky than fruity, with a pleasant tart flavour. It was nice but not very delicious. I would love to see more fruity drinks at Starbucks such as bananas.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ice
  • cup water
  • 1/2 cups strawberries
  • cup vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract or vanilla syrup
  • Whipped Cream (optional)

How Is It Made?

Preparing the puree sauce is the first step to making a strawberry creme frappuccino Starbucks drink. Here is how to make the strawberry puree.To make This frappuccino in a blender, you’ll need to add:Strawberry syrup, Milk, Ice, Vanilla ice cream .Then, blend all the ingredients until you get a smooth structure. Start on low and gradually turn it up. Add Greek yogurt for an extra creamy texture.Strawberry cream frappuccino usually has whipped cream on top, but you can have a delicious homemade cream frappuccino without the whipped cream too. Furthermore, you can make a cotton candy frappuccino if you like raspberry syrup. Pour the blended drink into a tall glass and top with whipped cream.
